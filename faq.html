Kimano FAQ

Have questions about Kimano? You came to the right place!

  • Kimano

    What is Kimano?

    Kimano is a blockchain technology company building bridging and security solutions to create a safer space in DeFi.

    What is the Kimano Ecosystem?

    The Kimano Ecosystem comprises a suite of dApps powered by Kimano, providing decentralized security solutions for projects and individuals in the DeFi space. Kimano will release many dApps: KimanoBridge, KimanoSwap,KimanoWallet and the Staking Lab available to both holders and projects.

    Is Kimano a coin or a token?

    Coins are the native asset of a blockchain. $Kimano is a token because it currently resides on Arbitrum network as a utility token.

    How is the $Kimano token used by the ecosystem of dApps?

    Kimano staking rewards include emission incentives plus a share of the protocol revenues, and can be boosted using locking periods

    What is the best way to stay updated about Kimano?

    Follow Kimano on Twitter, Facebook, Instagram, YouTube and the Kimano e-mail newsletter to stay up to date with everything going on with Kimano. You can also join in the discussion with other Kimano holders on Telegram, Discord,  and Reddit.

    What is DeFi?

    Decentralized finance, or DeFi, is an alternative to traditional financial systems, open to anyone, powered by the blockchain. DeFi takes out the middleman and gives individuals more control over their finances.

    What is a dApp?

    Decentralized applications, or dApps, are applications with code on the blockchain. dApps run on smart contracts that have been deployed to the blockchain and are not subject to any centralized, controlling party. Anyone is able to interact with dApps at any time from any device with an internet connection.

  • KimanoBridge

    What Are The Fees To Transfer Tokens On KimanoBridge?

    Transfers of the Kimano token on KimanoBridge are not subject to the transaction fee. You only pay gas fees which are calculated by the blockchains depending on congestion.

    What Can Be Transferred On KimanoBridge?

    Currently, only Kimano. We will be adding the native coins transfers (BNB, ETH, and MATIC) in the Q4 2021 - Q1 2022.

    How Can I Transfer Other Tokens Using KimanoBridge?

    If you are interested in transferring other tokens using KimanoBridge, talk to the developer of the project! We can work with them to get their project up and running to take advantage of access to multiple blockchains.

    Learn more about KimanoBridge at /bridge.html

  • STAKING LAB

    Can I stake my Kimano tokens on a different chain than what I am currently holding on?

    Using KimanoBridge, you can bridge your Kimano tokens between Ethereum, BNB Chain, Polygon, Avalanche, and Fantom networks. After bridging your tokens, you will be able to stake on the new chain.

    How many rewards will I receive by staking Kimano Token?

    There is no way to know the amount of rewards you will receive when you initiate your stake. The rewards a Kimano staker receives depend on how many tokens they stake and for how long

    What Token's can I earn from staking Kimano Token?

    You can earn token rewards by staking Kimano based on the length and amount you stake. This will be calculated based on the number of months you stake

    Learn more about the STAKING LAB at /staking

  • KimanoSwap

    With What Decentralized Exchanges Will KimanoSwap Work Upon Launch?

    KimanoSwap will work with UniSwap on Ethereum (ARB & ETH), PancakeSwap on BNB Chain (BSC), QuickSwap on Polygon (MATIC), TraderJoe on Avalanche (AVAX), and SpookySwap on Fantom (FTM).

    Will I Be Able To Swap Any Token With KimanoSwap?

    You will be able to Swap any token that is listed at PancakeSwap (BSC), QuickSwap (Polygon) and UniSwap (Ethereum). Currently you can swap our native Kimano token.

    How Will KimanoSwap Help To Secure My Crypto Holdings?

    Some tokens have been created with bad intentions, authorizing the token for swap can give the contract access to the rest of your wallet. KimanoSwap will be integrated with KimanoWallet so that confirming a swap will never give a contract full access to the rest of your holdings. If you are worried about tokens you have interacted with in the past, you can check your Token Approvals on BSCScan: bscscan.com/tokenapprovalchecker

  • KimanoWallet

    How Will KimanoWallet Help Protect Me Against Dusting Attacks?

    KimanoWallet will act as a “firewall” in front of your wallet, residing on the blockchain. Unknown tokens that are sent to your address carry a higher risk of being part of a dusting attack and will be hidden by default.

    Why Does Kimano Want To Build KimanoWallet?

    No - KimanoWallet does not require nor have access to your seed phrase (private key). Never share your seed phrase with anyone, including Kimano team members. We, as well as our websites and dApps, will never ask for your seed phrase. Individuals and websites that do ask for your seed phrase are scam(mers).

    What Security Features Will KimanoWallet Have?

    Since KimanoWallet is still in development, the exact security features are being worked out. However, your assets will be protected in a similar way to how the bridge vault works to protect the Kimano supply to power KimanoBridge. KimanoWallet will also have the feature to add a secondary/backup wallet access in case you lose your private keys.
