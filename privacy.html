Kimano Protocol Privacy Policy

The Privacy Policy explains how your personal details are gathered, utilized, and shared when you purchase from or visit the Site.


PERSONAL INFO OBTAINED


We obtain specific information about your device automatically when you access the Site. This includes details about your web browser, IP address, time zone, and some cookies installed on your device. While you explore the Site, we also collect information about the particular web pages or products you view, the search terms or websites that directed you to the Site, and how you interact with it. This information that is collected automatically is referred to as "Device Information."


DEVICE INFO OBTAINED


  • Cookies are files that store data on your computer or device, and usually have an anonymous unique identifier. If you want more information about cookies or how to disable them, you can visit http://www.allaboutcookies.org.
  • Log files keep track of your actions on the Site, including your IP address, browser type, Internet service provider, referring/exit pages, and date/time stamps.
  • Web beacons," "tags," and "pixels" are electronic files that record information about your browsing behavior, such as which sections you visit, what you click on, and how long you stay on certain pages.

When you purchase a product from our Site, we collect certain information from you, including your name, billing and shipping address, payment information (including credit card numbers and crypto wallet addresses), email address, and phone number. This information is referred to as "Order Information" and is standard in the ecommerce industry to fulfill your order.


In this Privacy Policy, when we refer to "Personal Information," we mean both Device Information and Order Information.


HOW WE UTILIZE PERSONAL INFO


We use the Order Information we collect to fulfill your orders (including processing payments, arranging for shipping, and providing order confirmations and invoices). We also use this information to communicate with you, screen orders for potential fraud, and send you information or advertising about our products or services based on your preferences.

The Device Information we collect helps us screen for potential fraud, improve and optimize our Site, and assess the success of our marketing campaigns. For example, we generate analytics about how our customers interact with the Site.


We share your Personal Information with third parties to help us use it as described above, such as with Google Analytics. We may also share your Personal Information to comply with laws and regulations, respond to a lawful request for information, or protect our rights under Singapore Law.


ADVERTISING

This passage explains that the website uses your personal information to provide targeted advertisements that they believe may interest you. It provides links for opting out of targeted advertising from Facebook, Google, and Bing. To unsubscribe from Kimano's emails, you can use the unsubscribe link provided in the email footer. The passage also states that they do not change their data collection practices when they receive a Do Not Track signal from your browser. If you are a European resident, you have the right to access, correct, update, or delete your personal information. They will retain your order information unless you request its deletion. The website and services are not intended for minors. The privacy policy may be updated from time to time, and contact information is provided for any questions or complaints.
